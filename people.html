People

PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR

Raquel Coelho

Raquel Coelho is an Assistant Professor of Emerging Technologies and Learning Sciences at Pitt's School of Computing and Information, with a joint appointment as Research Scientist at the Learning Research and Development Center (LRDC) and a secondary appointment at the School of Education. She holds a PhD in Learning Sciences and Technology Design from Stanford University, is a founding member of the Learning Sciences Brazil Affiliate of the International Society of the Learning Sciences, and serves as Director of the Network of Academic Programs in the Learning Sciences (NAPLeS). Outside of research, Raquel enjoys running, music, and being in the moment.

GRADUATE STUDENTS

Ye Deng

Ye Eden Deng is a first-year PhD student in the Information Science program at the University of Pittsburgh. Her research interests lie at the intersection of learning sciences and human-AI interaction. She is particularly interested in how feedback, self-regulated learning, and intelligent tools shape learning experiences.
